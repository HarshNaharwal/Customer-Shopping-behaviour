# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.
The goal is to transform raw retail data into meaningful insights that help businesses make better decisions.

* **Python (Pandas):** Data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA)
* **SQL:** Solving business questions and extracting insights 
* **Power BI:** Building an interactive dashboard for visualization

---

## ❗ Business Problem

Retail companies often struggle to understand:

* Which products drive the most revenue
* Whether discounts are actually effective
* Who their most valuable customers are
* How customer segments behave differently

Without this understanding, businesses risk:

* Poor marketing strategies
* Low customer retention
* Reduced profitability

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Project

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze customer purchasing patterns
* Identify high-performing products and categories
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions
* Segment customers for better targeting
* Build a dashboard to support data-driven decisions

---

## 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

### 🔹 Key Metrics (Top Section)

* **Total Customers:** 5K
* **Unique Items:** 30
* **Average Review:** 3.62
* **Total Spend:** 994.18K
* **Average Spend:** 198.84

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

---

### 🔹 Revenue Analysis

* **By Gender:** Male customers contribute slightly more revenue than others
* **By Category:** Electronics generate the highest revenue
* **By Season:** Spring and Winter show the highest sales

---

### 🔹 Customer Behavior Insights

* **Shipping Type:** Standard and Express shipping have higher order volumes and spending
* **Payment Method:** Debit cards and Cash are most used
* **Age Group:** Customers aged **51+ contribute the highest revenue share**

---

### 🔹 Product Insights

* **Top Rated Products:** Jewelry, Pants, and Blouse
* **Top Revenue Products:** Headphones, Phone, and Bags
* **Low Rated Products:** Phones and Headphones (despite high sales → improvement opportunity)

---

### 🔹 Location Insights

* **Top Locations:** New York, Los Angeles, Phoenix
* **Low Revenue Locations:** Smaller regions like Montana, Idaho

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📈 **Electronics dominate revenue**, making them the most important category
* 💸 **Discounts do not always guarantee higher spending**, so they should be optimized
* 👥 **Male customers slightly outspend other segments**
* 🎯 **Older customers (51+) are the highest revenue contributors**
* 🚚 **Faster shipping correlates with higher purchase value**
* ⭐ **Some high-selling products have low ratings**, indicating quality or expectation gaps
* 🔁 **Repeat and loyal customers are key for long-term revenue**

---

## 💼 Business Impact

This analysis helps businesses:

* **Increase Revenue**

  * Focus on high-performing categories and products
  * Promote top-selling items

* **Improve Marketing Strategy**

  * Target high-value customer segments (age, gender, location)
  * Personalize campaigns

* **Optimize Pricing & Discounts**

  * Avoid unnecessary discounts
  * Identify discount-sensitive products

* **Enhance Customer Retention**

  * Identify loyal and repeat customers
  * Improve subscription strategies

* **Improve Customer Experience**

  * Fix low-rated products
  * Align product quality with customer expectations
